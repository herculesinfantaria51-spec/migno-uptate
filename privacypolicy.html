Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our applications — including flashcard apps, dictionary apps, and the routine planner app — collect, use, and protect your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to full transparency regarding how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as added words, created flashcards, registered routines, or any content you manually input. We may also collect basic technical data from your device, such as model and operating system, used solely to ensure proper app functionality.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Some information may be collected automatically, including crash reports, performance data, and anonymous identifiers used for internal analytics or non-personalized advertising.

3. How We Use the Information

Collected information is used to improve performance, personalize features, ensure correct functionality, and fix technical issues. No personal information is ever sold or shared for commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data may be stored locally on the device or in cloud services depending on the app’s features. We apply security measures to protect your information, including encryption when appropriate.

5. Information Sharing

Data may only be shared when required by law or with essential services that support app functionality (such as backup or analytics services). When possible, shared data is anonymized.

6. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Some apps may display non-personalized ads. Services such as Google Play Services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

7. User Rights

Users may request data removal, clarification, or additional details about data handling at any time.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the app to operate correctly. If the user uninstalls the app, all locally stored information is automatically removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will always display the most recent modification date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or support requests, please use the support email provided within the app.
