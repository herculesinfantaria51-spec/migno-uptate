Refund Policy

Since our products are digital software delivered via instant download, we generally do not offer refunds once the purchase is completed and the file has been accessed.

Exceptions:

  • Technical Issues: If the app does not work on your device and our support team cannot fix it.
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally bought the same app twice.

To request a refund under these exceptions, please contact us at your support email within 7 days of purchase.
